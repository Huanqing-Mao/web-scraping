                                            <p>In each of the following questions, two statements are given followed by three or four conclusions numbered I, II, III and IV. You have to take the given statements to be true even if they seem to be at variance from the commonly known facts and then decide which of the given conclusions logically follows from the given statements disregarding commonly known facts.</p>

<p><b>Conclusions:</b></p>

<li>All branches are leaves.</li> 
<li>All leaves are branches.</li> 
<li>All flowers are branches.</li> 
<li>Some leaves are branches.</li> 
</ol><p></p></div>
